🎯 Strategic Planning

A formalized project charter and actionable improvement measures derived directly from the analytical findings.

Project Charter: Project Catalyst

Initiative to maximize CLV and minimize funnel friction based on dataset parameters.

Objective

To utilize behavioral and transactional data to identify key bottlenecks in the e-commerce funnel, specifically targeting high cart abandonment rates, and to deploy UX and marketing interventions aiming to increase Average Order Value (AOV) and overall revenue.

Scope & Stakeholders

  • In Scope: Mobile checkout redesign, automated cart recovery systems, loyalty tier structuring.
  • Out of Scope: Supply chain logistics, backend inventory management systems.
Sponsor: CMO
Execution: UX & Dev
Analysis: Data Team
Review: Q3 End

Prescribed Measures of Improvement

🛒

Cart Recovery Automation

Priority: HIGH

Data Context: Cart abandonment acts as a significant revenue blocker.

Action: Implement a multi-stage triggered email and SMS sequence at 1hr, 12hr, and 24hr marks post-abandonment, offering tiered incentives based on basket value.

📱

Mobile Friction Reduction

Priority: HIGH

Data Context: Mobile devices dictate a massive portion of ordering sessions.

Action: Audit the mobile checkout DOM. Integrate Apple Pay and Google Pay to bypass manual form entry. Reduce required form fields by 30% for guest checkouts.

⭐

CLV Tier Gamification

Priority: MED

Data Context: Significant revenue potential exists in nurturing lower/mid CLV tiers.

Action: Launch a progressive loyalty program. Offer exclusive early access to top categories to incentivize Medium CLV users to reach High CLV spend thresholds.
