The Big Mac Index Explorer

Welcome to the interactive dashboard for "Burgernomics." Based on the theory of purchasing-power parity (PPP), this index uses the price of a McDonald's Big Mac as a benchmark to determine whether international currencies are overvalued or undervalued relative to the US Dollar. Use the slider below to journey through time and observe how global currency valuations have shifted from 1986 to 2025.
